About

Indigenous Peoples of Bicol

The Bicol Region is home to diverse Indigenous Peoples, including the Agta of Mt. Isarog and Mt. Malinao and communities around Mt. Bulusan. Their histories, oral traditions, weaving, music, and ecological knowledge are vital to the region’s identity.

This hub gathers verified references, community contributions, and multimedia resources to amplify voices, protect rights, and encourage respectful engagement.

Core Principles

  • Community consent and attribution
  • Cultural sensitivity and accuracy
  • Open knowledge with safeguards
  • Respect for land, waters, and ancestors
