Policy

Privacy & Content Policy

This policy protects Indigenous communities, knowledge holders, contributors, visitors, and the integrity of cultural heritage shared on this hub. We are committed to ethical, respectful, and accurate representation of Indigenous languages, traditions, and knowledge, particularly those of Philippine Indigenous Peoples (IPs) such as the Agta groups in the Bicol Region.

Consent & Sensitive Information

Contributors and visitors must not share sensitive personal, cultural, or sacred information (e.g., secret/sacred knowledge, traditional practices restricted by community protocols, or personally identifiable details of community members) without free, prior, and informed consent from the relevant Indigenous knowledge holders or community. We encourage users to follow principles of respect: obtain community approval before documenting or uploading content involving Indigenous languages, oral traditions, images, or locations.

Storage & Security

We use secure practices (e.g., encryption for data in transit, limited access) to protect information. Agreement status (e.g., consent to terms) is stored in local browser storage to minimize repeated prompts and enhance user experience. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing or unrelated purposes. Data is used solely to maintain the site, facilitate contributions, and improve cultural preservation efforts.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We comply with applicable laws, including Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) where relevant.

Content Guidelines

To ensure the hub remains a respectful space for sharing and preserving Indigenous heritage:

Accuracy & Respect

All content must be accurate, culturally sensitive, and based on verifiable sources or direct community input. Misrepresentation of Indigenous languages, histories, or practices is not permitted.

Cultural Sensitivity and Consent

Content involving Indigenous Peoples (e.g., languages like Isarog Agta/Inagta Partido, oral narratives, photos/videos of communities, sacred sites, or traditional knowledge) requires:

  • Free, prior, and informed consent from the community or knowledge holders.
  • Adherence to any community protocols (e.g., restrictions on sharing certain knowledge).

Prohibited Content

  • Content that exploits, stereotypes, or harms Indigenous communities.
  • Unauthorized commercial use of cultural expressions or traditional knowledge.
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or offensive material toward any group.

Moderation & Reporting

We review submissions for compliance. Users can report concerns about content via [contact method/email]. Violations may result in removal of content, account suspension, or other actions.

Intellectual & Cultural Property

We support Indigenous rights to control their cultural heritage and traditional knowledge (aligned with UNDRIP Article 31 and Philippine IPRA/RA 8371). Contributors retain ownership of their submissions, but by posting, grant the hub a non-exclusive license to display and archive for preservation/educational purposes only.
