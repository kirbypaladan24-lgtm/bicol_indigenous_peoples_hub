Policy

Privacy & Content Policy

This policy helps protect Indigenous communities, contributors, and visitors while keeping the hub respectful and accurate.

Privacy

  • We collect only essential account information needed to publish or manage content.
  • Please do not share sensitive personal data without consent.
  • We store agreement status in local storage to avoid repeated prompts.

Content & Safety

  • Respectful language is required. No harassment, hate, or abuse.
  • Share accurate information and cite sources when possible.
  • Upload only media you own or have permission to share.
  • Locations should be shared responsibly and with cultural sensitivity.
